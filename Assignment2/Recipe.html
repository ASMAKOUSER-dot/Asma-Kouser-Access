Manchuri

Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • onion
  • Cauliflower
  • tomato sauce
  • capsicum
  • soya sauce
  • cooking oil

instructions

  1. Mix corn flour and finely chopped cauliflower and fry in cooking oil into small balls
  2. in a separate pan fry capsicum, onion, tomato sauce and soya sauce for 4 minutes with 2 spoons of cooking oil
  3. Add separately fried balls inside the another mixture and mix well
